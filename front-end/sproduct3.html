Product Details

An exquisite Indian granite, Blue Oceano has a cream background with blue and dark grey colour speckles spread in a wave pattern, making it resemble the ocean. It consists of red dots that give this granite a pop of colour, enhancing its functionality and hence making it a highlighting element of any place. Due to this colour scheme, it could be used for both interior and exterior applications, especially for flooring, wall cladding, vanity tops, countertops, backsplashes, staircase and many more. Granite has the quality of being moisture-resistant and stain-resistant, which makes it appropriate for places like the kitchen and restrooms. It can withstand extreme weather conditions, so it is an apt stone for outdoor application. You can avail of this premium quality stone in form of blocks, slabs and tiles from Madhav Marble & Granite Ltd. We are among the top granite suppliers and exporters of natural stones from India.
